GOOD BOOKKEEPING QR CODE SETUP
=================================

The Good Bookkeeping referral partner card requires the QR code image to be placed in this directory.

Required file: good-bookkeeping-qr.jpg

The QR code image has been provided and should be saved with this exact filename in the /public directory.

Once saved, the Sponsors section will display:
- The QR code prominently at the top of the card
- Text explaining it connects to Rocky Top Drivers for CFO/bookkeeping services
- A "Schedule Appointment" button
- A link to goodbookkeeping.com

The card matches the existing sponsor card design with:
- Dark navy background
- Gold accent borders
- Centered layout
- Responsive behavior
